Getting To Know Our Animals

Bears

Bears are mammals with large bodies, shaggy hair, and short tails. They are omnivorous, meaning they eat both plants and animals. Bears are found in North and South America, and Eurasia.

    • Giraffes

    Giraffes are the world's tallest mammals, with long necks and legs that can reach up to 18 feet tall. They have short bodies, tufted tails, and short manes. Their coats are usually gray, white, or tan with brown patches that help them camouflage in the savannah.
